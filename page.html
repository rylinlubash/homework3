My Baking



I’ve been baking since I was a child, and my favorite things to bake are the most technical complex recipes. I also have a major sweet tooth, so I make almost exclusively desserts. When I was younger, I got really into watching YouTubers like Claire Saffitz and Sally’s Baking Addiction and learning about how the recipes work and why. I found this fascinating, and the skills I learned help me to make more complex baked goods. I am always pushing my skills to make some new recipe for a pastry I haven’t tried before. This was partially driven by my mom being a terrible baker. While most people who like to bake learned how to from their mom, I figured out how to bake to stop her from ignoring recipes and timers and burning cakes.


When I’m baking something quickly for comfort, I usually make chocolate chip bar cookies, brownies, or banana bread. However, I find it the most fun and rewarding to find fancy complex recipes and try to make them. This way I get to learn about how my favorite desserts are made.
